About Happy Moments - Wedding Decorators,
 Pallikaranai, 
Chennai

Happy Moments is a wedding decoration company based in Chennai. Founded by Mahalakshmi, Happy Moments have been creating various themes and designing decor for weddings since 2023.



About the team of Happy Moments

While the company started recently in 2023, they are slowly but surely creating memories for couples and their families with their talents.



Services provided by Happy Moments

They provide a customized decor package for weddings, engagement, reception and other functions and provide services including

Flowers
Audio Visuals

Mandap
Tents
