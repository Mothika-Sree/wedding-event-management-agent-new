What are the things to be considered before booking a wedding caterer in Chennai?

Here are the things that you should tend to before hiring catering services in Chennai.

Gather enough details: The first thing to do is to use the internet to find out as much information as possible about the catering service provider you are considering. When you search online, you will run into many wedding caterers in Chennai. However, only a few of them strive to offer high-quality food at economic prices.
The menu: Since wedding food is where the soul of Indian weddings resides, you can't trifle with it. You can request a custom menu specifically created for your guests or try a preset one. The right providers of food catering in Chennai will always have an inbuilt menu that they present before their clients. Once you go through the menu, you can customize the items included. You should also look for service providers that can prepare both Indian and continental dishes with equal quality.
Discuss the venue: Some people plan their event in open gardens or lawns or resorts. If you choose a wedding venue in Chennai, then you must find out if the providers of catering services in Chennai can work at such a location. Always check the previous experience of the caterer when it comes to choosing service providers.
The ability to respond to requirements: Cheapest prices aren't the only thing that should attract you. The service provider of wedding catering in Chennai should be able to respond promptly to your requirements. Their responsiveness will tell you whether they're worthy enough for your event or not.
Get a taste: Always try to taste their food, and if possible samples from the menu you are choosing. Many people try to avoid doing it, but you shouldn't. If you don't taste a sample, then you won't know anything about the quality and taste of the food that the caterer will serve on the day of the event.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do caterers in Chennai charge for a wedding function?

Catering charges depend upon how many dishes you have selected and per plate cost starts from 1k and goes up to 3k. Usually, wedding food companies offer a package deal from which you can choose the dishes, but you can always add more dishes and pay accordingly.

Do banquet halls in Chennai provide catering services as well?

Of course, a few banquet halls have their own catering services that you can avail. If you hire them, then you can reduce the complications of coordinating catering with the venue. However, you must discuss the matter in advance. Otherwise, the owner of the banquet hall may send the catering team to manage another event.

What are some interesting food trends we spotted in Chennai weddings?
Brunch: Brunch has become popular lately because daytime weddings are cheaper than doing dinnertime. Brunch themed weddings are comparatively less stressful and gives your wedding a casual elegance.
Think fresh: People are turning to fresh and organic foods sourced locally to give their wedding a unique and different feel.
Get involved: A chef getting involved with the guests is the best delight for anyone. People really like the idea when the chef comes to the table to serve the guests and describes the cuisines.
Go Rustic: Rustic weddings are very popular right now. The food is usually served in family style with buffet tables instead of round tables.
Drink up: Personalised beverage stations are very popular these days. People have a thing for beverage stations whether they can get drinks which can be alcoholic or non-alcoholic.
What services are included along with preparation of food by wedding caterers in Chennai?

Different caterers provide different services but some common ones are:

menu designing
cuisine preparation and food styling
clean-up crew staffing.
Should I book different wedding catering services for different functions or the same one?

It is suitable to book the same marriage caterers as it will help you with the total cost. If you want something which one catering company can’t provide then you can always look for other options.

What are the mistakes to avoid when booking a wedding caterer in Chennai?

The best way to ensure that you get excellent service on the day of the wedding is to avoid the following mistakes.

License: Indeed, every company of food catering in Chennai should possess a license. Most people tend to forget to check whether the service provider has the permit to cater to weddings due to all the hassles associated with arranging the event.
Experience: If you're planning to serve both Indian and continental cuisines, then you must find out whether the catering company has enough experience to prepare the desired dishes. Apart from that, you also need to know how long the company has been in business.
Trained staff: Many catering firms hire inexperienced youngsters who make mistakes while serving guests. Therefore, you should try to find out if the company of wedding catering in Chennai employs experienced staff members who are well-versed in serving wedding food to the multitudes of guests.
