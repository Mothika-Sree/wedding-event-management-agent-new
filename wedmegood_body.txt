About Kailash Resort - Wedding Venues,
 ECR East Coast Road, 
Chennai

Offering marvellous venue areas as well as relaxing accommodations, Kailash Resort is situated in Chennai of Tamil Nadu. It began serving officially in 2011 and has delighted clients with its modern day facilities, blissful atmosphere and rich green environment.



Space/Capacity available at Kailash Resort
Indoor banquet hall- 1000 seating and 1250 floating capacity
Outdoor lawn- 700 seating and 1000 floating capacity
26 rooms


Facilities available at Kailash Resort

The facilities here include

Ample parking space
Outside catering permitted
Inhouse decorators
Inhouse and outside dj


Location of Kailash Resort

The resort is in ECR East Coast Road of Chennai, Tamil Nadu.



Cuisines at Kailash Resort

With the flexibility to bring outside caterers, this venue allows clients to have a menu that suits their preferences and gathering.
